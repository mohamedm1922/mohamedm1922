## 👋 About Me

**Most analysts see data. I see the customer behind it.**

I started my career in customer-facing roles before transitioning into analytics — and that background changes everything. I don't just analyse numbers, I understand the *behaviour* behind them. I've worked on both sides: handling real customer queries in high-pressure environments, and then building dashboards that explain *why* those issues happen in the first place.

At **Sheffield Hallam University**, I deepened this approach through projects involving SQL-based customer analysis and Power BI dashboards tracking live performance metrics. Alongside that, I co-direct **Sabr and Stride CIC** — a community-focused organisation where I apply data tracking and KPI reporting to measure real programme outcomes.

I approach every dataset with one key question: **What decision should this data support?**

> 🎯 Currently seeking **Data Analyst roles** focused on business insights, customer analytics, and data-driven decision-making. Open to opportunities — feel free to connect.
